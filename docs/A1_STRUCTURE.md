# Deutsch Lernen A1 - Complete Learning Structure

## 🎯 App Overview
This app is designed specifically for **A1 German learners** with four main learning paths:
1. **🔤 Nouns: Der, Die, Das** - Master German noun genders
2. **📚 Grammar A1** - Essential verb conjugations and sentence structure
3. **📖 Vocabulary A1** - Build vocabulary foundation
4. **🗣️ Speaking (Sprechen)** - Practice speaking German

---

## 📚 Lesson Structure

### 1️⃣ NOUNS: DER, DIE, DAS (4 Lessons)
These lessons teach German articles and noun genders in the nominative case.

#### Lesson 1: Nominativ - Der, Die, Das Grundlagen
- **Objective**: Understand the three genders of German nouns
- **Content**: 
  - Masculine nouns use **DER**
  - Feminine nouns use **DIE**
  - Neuter nouns use **DAS**
  - Plural always uses **DIE**
- **Exercises**: 
  - Multiple choice (identify article)
  - Fill-in-the-blank (correct gender)
- **Duration**: 15 minutes

#### Lesson 2: Common Masculine Nouns (DER)
- **Examples**: der Mann, der Stuhl, der Hut, der Kaffee, der Bleistift
- **Tips**: Many end in -er, -el, -en
- **Exercises**:
  - Matching articles with nouns
  - Fill-in-the-blank with masculine nouns
- **Duration**: 12 minutes

#### Lesson 3: Common Feminine Nouns (DIE)
- **Examples**: die Frau, die Tür, die Schule, die Farbe, die Hand
- **Tips**: Most end in -e, -in, -tion, -heit
- **Exercises**:
  - Multiple choice (feminine vs others)
  - Sentence completion
- **Duration**: 12 minutes

#### Lesson 4: Common Neuter Nouns (DAS)
- **Examples**: das Kind, das Haus, das Buch, das Auge, das Papier
- **Tips**: Many start with Ge-, end in -chen, -lein
- **Exercises**:
  - Multiple choice (identify neuter)
  - Fill-in-the-blank practice
- **Duration**: 12 minutes

---

### 2️⃣ GRAMMAR A1 (4 Lessons)
Master essential German verbs and grammar structures.

#### Lesson 1: Verb Conjugation - SEIN (To Be)
- **Conjugation table**:
  - ich bin (I am)
  - du bist (you are)
  - er/sie/es ist (he/she/it is)
  - wir sind (we are)
  - ihr seid (you all are)
  - sie/Sie sind (they are / formal you)
- **Exercises**: Conjugation drills, sentence completion
- **Duration**: 13 minutes

#### Lesson 2: Verb Conjugation - HABEN (To Have)
- **Conjugation table**:
  - ich habe
  - du hast
  - er/sie/es hat
  - wir haben
  - ihr habt
  - sie/Sie haben
- **Exercises**: Usage examples, conjugation practice
- **Duration**: 13 minutes

#### Lesson 3: Regular Verbs (Machen, Spielen, Lernen)
- **Pattern**: Stem + ending (-e, -st, -t, -en, -t, -en)
- **Examples**:
  - machen: ich mache, du machst, er macht...
  - spielen: ich spiele, du spielst, er spielt...
  - lernen: ich lerne, du lernst, er lernt...
- **Exercises**: Pattern recognition, verb conjugation
- **Duration**: 14 minutes

#### Lesson 4: Personal Pronouns & Nominative Case
- **All pronouns with examples**:
  - ich (I) - Ich bin müde
  - du (you) - Du bist nett
  - er (he), sie (she), es (it)
  - wir (we), ihr (you pl), sie (they), Sie (formal)
- **Exercises**: Pronoun replacement, subject identification
- **Duration**: 11 minutes

---

### 3️⃣ VOCABULARY A1 (3 Lessons)
Build essential German vocabulary for beginners.

#### Lesson 1: Family Members
- **Vocabulary**:
  - der Vater (father)
  - die Mutter (mother)
  - der Sohn (son)
  - die Tochter (daughter)
  - der Bruder (brother)
  - die Schwester (sister)
  - der Großvater (grandfather)
  - die Großmutter (grandmother)
  - der Onkel (uncle), die Tante (aunt)
  - der Cousin, die Cousine
- **Exercises**: Vocabulary matching, fill-in-the-blank
- **Duration**: 10 minutes

#### Lesson 2: Numbers & Time
- **Numbers 0-20**: null, eins, zwei, drei... zwanzig
- **Days of week**: Montag - Sonntag
- **Time expressions**: 
  - Wie spät ist es? (What time is it?)
  - die Stunde (hour)
  - die Minute (minute)
- **Exercises**: Number recognition, day spelling
- **Duration**: 12 minutes

#### Lesson 3: Colors & Adjectives
- **Colors**: rot, blau, grün, gelb, schwarz, weiß, grau, orange, rosa, braun
- **Common adjectives**:
  - groß/klein (big/small)
  - neu/alt (new/old)
  - heiß/kalt (hot/cold)
  - schnell/langsam (fast/slow)
  - jung/alt (young/old)
- **Exercises**: Color matching, adjective usage
- **Duration**: 11 minutes

---

### 4️⃣ SPEAKING - SPRECHEN (2 Lessons)
Practice speaking German with confidence.

#### Lesson 1: Greetings & Introductions
- **Basic greetings**:
  - Guten Morgen! (Good morning)
  - Guten Tag! (Hello)
  - Guten Abend! (Good evening)
  - Auf Wiedersehen! (Goodbye)
- **Introductions**:
  - Ich heiße... (My name is...)
  - Ich bin aus... (I'm from...)
  - Wie geht es dir? (How are you?)
  - Es geht mir gut. (I'm fine)
- **Exercises**: Phrase typing, pronunciation practice
- **Duration**: 13 minutes

#### Lesson 2: Common Phrases & Questions
- **Questions**:
  - Wie heißt du? (What's your name?)
  - Woher kommst du? (Where are you from?)
  - Was machst du? (What do you do?)
  - Hast du eine Familie? (Do you have a family?)
- **Statements**:
  - Ich bin Schüler/Schülerin (I'm a student)
  - Ich arbeite als... (I work as...)
  - Ich wohne in... (I live in...)
  - Meine Hobbys sind... (My hobbies are...)
- **Exercises**: Phrase completion, conversation simulation
- **Duration**: 14 minutes

## 🎓 Exercise Types Used

1. **Multiple Choice**: Select the correct answer from options

3. **Matching**: Connect vocabulary with definitions
4. **Typing**: Write out complete phrases and sentences

6. **Speaking** (Future): Record and compare pronunciation
